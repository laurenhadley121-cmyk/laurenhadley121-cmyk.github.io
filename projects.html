College Counseling Work

I support 9th through 12th grade students with the college application process. While I am enrolled in graduate school,I am accepting a limited number of clients.

What I Offer

  • College application strategy and planning
  • Personal statement writing and editing
  • Interview preparation and practice

Approach

I believe the college application process can be an opportunity for self-discovery and growth. My approach is student-centered, focusing on helping each individual articulate their unique story and aspirations. I aim to create a supportive space where students can explore their interests, values, and goals as they navigate this important transition. I have worked with students planning to attend a wide variety of institutions, including highly selective universities, liberal arts colleges, and state schools. I am passionate about helping students find the right fit for their academic and personal growth.

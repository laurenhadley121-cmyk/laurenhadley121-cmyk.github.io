About Me

I am currently in the process of becoming a therapist and deeply value the quiet, reflective work of listening. My path is shaped by both formal training and everyday moments of curiosity, compassion, and connection.

This space is where I collect those moments — a place to share what I am learning, how my college counseling work is growing me, and the creative inspiration I return to when I need perspective.

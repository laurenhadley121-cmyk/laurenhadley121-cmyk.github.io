Therapy Services

Approach to Therapy

I approach therapy with a focus on creating a safe and supportive environment for my clients. My hope is to help clients discover who they truly are and leave sessions with a growing sense of joy, self-love, and confidence in who they are becoming.

Therapy Coursework

I am currently in my internship year at Thomas Jefferson University where I am completing my Master's in Couples and Family Therapy. Some examples of completed coursework include:

  • Theory and Practice of Family Therapy
  • Theory and Practice of Couples Therapy
  • Intro to Sex Therapy
  • Human Sexuality Studies
  • Assessment and Mental Health Diagnosis and Treatment

I came to this work through my prior experience with students and families in educational settings.

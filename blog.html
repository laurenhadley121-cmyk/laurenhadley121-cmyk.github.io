Reflections on Therapy

Approach to Therapy

[Date]

I approach therapy with a focus on creating a safe and supportive environment for my clients. I work to build trust and rapport, ensuring that each individual feels heard and understood. I approach therapy through a systemic lens, considering the interconnectedness of individuals within their families and communities. My hope is to help clients discover who they truly are and leave sessions with a growing sense of joy, self-love, and confidence in who they are becoming.
